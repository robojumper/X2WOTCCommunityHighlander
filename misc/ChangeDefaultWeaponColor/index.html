ChangeDefaultWeaponColor

Tracking Issue: #397

Tags: customization

Soldiers with randomly generated appearance get the beige weapon color by default (color number 20). This change moves the default weapon color number to XComContent.ini, where it can be changed by mods or by the player manually.

[XComGame.XGCharacterGenerator]
iDefaultWeaponTint = 20
